# Private 3D model library

Canonical: https://stl-shelf.com/private-3d-model-library

A private library for your 3D models: searchable, versioned, and previewable. Built for makers who want ownership and clarity.
STL Shelf is designed for makers who want complete control of their own model archive without social feeds or public distribution.

## The problem

- Many model tools are built around discovery and sharing, not private organization.
- That makes personal libraries feel secondary and hard to maintain.
- When ownership and clarity matter, you need a focused private workflow.

## Simple workflow

1. **Keep all files in one private space**: Store your models in a personal archive built for search and structure.
2. **Use tags and metadata**: Create practical organization by project, part type, and print status.
3. **Maintain versions and preview**: Track change history and inspect models before printing.

## What STL Shelf is / is not

### STL Shelf is

- A private library for your STL, 3MF, and OBJ files
- Searchable tags that keep projects and parts organized
- Version history so every revision stays traceable
- Browser preview before you send models to print

### STL Shelf is not

- No marketplace
- No sharing or social feed
- No import or sync from other services

## FAQ

### Is STL Shelf a marketplace?

No. STL Shelf is focused on private model organization.

### Can I share models publicly from STL Shelf?

No. Sharing and social features are intentionally excluded.

### What is the main benefit of a private library?

You keep ownership, structure, and version clarity as your archive grows.

### Can I migrate data in from other services automatically?

No. STL Shelf does not provide import or sync connectors.
