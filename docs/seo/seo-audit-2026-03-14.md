## Internal linking map

### Pillar support

- Current organization pages should point to `/how-to-organize-stl-files` and `/stl-file-management-software`.
- Current private/archive pages should point to `/private-3d-model-library-software`.
- Current self-hosted/open-source pages should point to `/self-hosted-3d-model-library-software` and `/open-source-3d-model-library-software`.

### Overlap that needs differentiation

- `/organize-stl-files` vs `/how-to-organize-stl-files`
  - Keep `/organize-stl-files` as the concise workflow pillar-adjacent page.
  - Keep `/how-to-organize-stl-files` as the deeper tutorial and main long-tail problem-solving page.
- `/stl-file-organizer` vs `/stl-file-management-software`
  - Keep `/stl-file-organizer` product-facing and query-aligned.
  - Keep `/stl-file-management-software` category-definitional.
- `/private-3d-model-library` vs `/private-3d-model-library-software`
  - Keep the former concept and ownership oriented.
  - Keep the latter category and software-buying oriented.
- `/version-control-for-stl-files` vs `/stl-version-control-for-makers`
  - Keep the former broader and category-level.
  - Keep the latter maker/reprint/use-case specific.
- `/tagging-system-for-3d-models` vs `/tagging-stl-files-for-fast-search`
  - Keep the former taxonomy/system design oriented.
  - Keep the latter implementation/search-speed oriented.
- Self-hosted/open-source pages
  - Differentiate by query framing: general self-hosted, STL-specific self-hosted, open-source category, organizer-specific, private/self-hosted, and software-category hybrid.
